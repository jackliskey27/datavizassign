01 — Overall trend

Average AI mention rate, all industries

Share of job postings referencing AI across all nine industries, aggregated by year.

The mention rate grew nearly fourfold between 2017 and 2025. The sharpest single-year jump occurred between 2021 and 2022 (+20.5pp), coinciding with the mainstream emergence of large language models. Growth has since plateaued, suggesting broad market saturation.

02 — Industry spotlight

Finance vs Tech — AI mention rate

The two industries with the highest sustained AI mention rates across the full period.

Finance Tech
Finance entered 2017 already elevated at 48.5%, reflecting early algorithmic hiring. Tech grew more steeply (+52.8pp), nearly converging with Finance by 2024. Both consistently outpaced the cross-industry average.

03 — 2025 snapshot

AI mention rate by industry — 2025

Ranked share of job postings mentioning AI for each of the nine industries in the most recent year.

Finance and Tech remain dominant. Education showed the strongest late-period surge, reaching 75% in 2025 — up from just 15.6% in 2017.
